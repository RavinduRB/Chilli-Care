General Questions

Chilli Care is an advanced AI-powered platform that helps farmers, agricultural researchers, and plant enthusiasts identify and diagnose diseases in chilli plants. Using state-of-the-art deep learning technology and computer vision, our system can detect five major chilli diseases with over 90% accuracy by analyzing plant leaf images.

Our system uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on thousands of labeled chilli plant images. Here's how it works:

  1. You upload a clear photo of a chilli plant leaf
  2. Our AI model processes the image and analyzes visual patterns
  3. The system compares features against its trained knowledge base
  4. Within seconds, you receive a diagnosis with confidence score and treatment recommendations

For enhanced accuracy, we also integrate with Google Gemini AI for additional image analysis and contextual recommendations.

Our system can accurately detect the following five conditions:

  • Healthy Leaves - Normal, disease-free plants
  • Anthracnose - Fungal disease causing dark lesions
  • Leaf Curl Virus - Viral infection causing leaf distortion
  • Whitefly Infestation - Pest damage affecting plant health
  • Yellowish Leaves - Nutrient deficiency or disease symptoms

Absolutely! Chilli Care is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can take a photo directly with your phone's camera or upload existing images from your gallery. The mobile experience is optimized for field use, making it perfect for farmers working in their fields.

For optimal accuracy, follow these guidelines when capturing images:

  • Lighting: Take photos in natural daylight, avoiding harsh shadows
  • Focus: Ensure the leaf is in sharp focus and fills most of the frame
  • Background: Use a plain or neutral background when possible
  • Leaf condition: Capture the affected area clearly
  • Angle: Take photos from directly above or at a slight angle
  • Image quality: Use high-resolution images (avoid blurry or pixelated photos)

Currently, there are no strict limits on the number of images you can analyze. However, we encourage responsible use of the service. If you need to analyze a large number of images for research or commercial purposes, please contact us to discuss appropriate usage plans.

Accuracy & Reliability

Our AI model achieves over 90% accuracy in controlled test conditions. The actual accuracy in real-world scenarios depends on factors like image quality, lighting conditions, disease progression stage, and plant variety. We provide a confidence score with each prediction to help you understand the reliability of the diagnosis. For critical decisions, we recommend consulting with agricultural experts and using our system as a supplementary diagnostic tool.

No. While Chilli Care provides highly accurate AI-powered predictions, it should be used as a diagnostic aid, not a replacement for professional agricultural advice. We recommend:

  • Using our system for quick initial assessments
  • Consulting with certified plant pathologists or agricultural extension officers for confirmation
  • Following up with laboratory testing when needed
  • Considering multiple factors including soil health, weather, and farming practices

While our system is highly accurate, no AI is perfect. If you believe a prediction is incorrect, please check the confidence score provided. Low confidence scores may indicate uncertainty. You can also try uploading a different or clearer image. We continuously improve our model, and user feedback helps us enhance accuracy. If you encounter persistent issues, please contact our support team with the image and details.
