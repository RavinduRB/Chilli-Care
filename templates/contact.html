Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the disease detection?

Our AI model achieves 95%+ accuracy in detecting chilli plant diseases, trained on thousands of real-world images and validated through extensive testing.

Is the service free to use?

Yes! Chilli Care is completely free for farmers and agricultural enthusiasts. Our mission is to support sustainable farming worldwide.

Can I use this on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. You can even use your phone's camera to capture images directly.

What should I do after getting a diagnosis?

Follow the treatment recommendations provided with your diagnosis. We offer both conventional and organic treatment options. For severe cases, consult with a local agricultural expert.
