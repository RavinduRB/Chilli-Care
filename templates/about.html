About Chilli Care

Leveraging cutting-edge deep learning to revolutionize agricultural disease detection

Our Mission

Chilli Care is an industry-level plant disease detection system designed to help farmers, agricultural researchers, and plant enthusiasts identify and treat chilli plant diseases with unprecedented accuracy and speed.

Built using state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and trained on thousands of real-world images, our system achieves 90%+ accuracy in detecting five major chilli diseases.

Technology Stack

Deep Learning

TensorFlow 2.x, Keras, Custom CNN architecture with BatchNormalization and Dropout

Backend

Flask, Python 3.8+, RESTful API, CORS-enabled

Frontend

Modern HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, Responsive Design

Detected Diseases

  • 1
    Whitefly Infestation

    Sap-sucking insects causing yellowing and stunted growth

  • 2
    Leaf Yellowing (Chlorosis)

    Nutrient deficiency or viral infections

  • 3
    Anthracnose

    Fungal disease causing fruit rot and yield loss

  • 4
    Leaf Curl Virus

    Devastating viral disease transmitted by whiteflies

  • ✓
    Healthy Plants

    Detection of disease-free chilli plants
